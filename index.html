03 / CYBER AWARENESS

Stay alert. Stay informed.

Understanding common threats is the first step toward preventing them.

✉

Phishing

Fake messages designed to steal your data. Check senders, avoid urgent links, and verify requests independently.

◉

Malware

Harmful software such as viruses, worms, spyware, and trojans. Download only from trusted sources.

⚠

Ransomware

Malware that locks files until a payment is demanded. Keep backups and update software regularly.

◎

Identity theft

Someone misuses your personal data. Protect OTPs, privacy settings, and sensitive documents.
